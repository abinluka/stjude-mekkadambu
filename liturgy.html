Sacraments

Contact the parish office to arrange any sacrament. Advance notice required.

💧

Baptism

Registration at least 2 weeks in advance. Preparation class mandatory for parents & godparents.

🕊️

First Communion

After completion of Catechism Grade 2. Annual celebration at the parish.

🙏

Confession

Every Saturday at 5:00 PM. Other times by appointment with the Vicar.

💒

Marriage

Banns published 3 Sundays before. Pre-marriage course required. Contact 6 months in advance.

🕯️

Anointing of the Sick

Available to the seriously ill and elderly. Contact the parish office or Vicar at any time.

✝️

Funeral Mass

Scheduled according to family arrangements. Contact the parish office immediately on bereavement.
