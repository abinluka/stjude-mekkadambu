Welcome to Our Parish

A Community of Faith & Fellowship

St. Jude Church (RCSC) at Mekkadambu is a vibrant Catholic parish under the Diocese of Muvattupuzha. We are a community united in faith, dedicated to worship, service, and the transforming love of Christ.

Founded in 1998, our parish has grown steadily in faith and numbers, nurturing generations of families through the sacraments, education, and community service.
