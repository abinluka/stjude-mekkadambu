Youth Ministry

KCYM — Kerala Catholic Youth Movement

KCYM is the vibrant youth wing of our parish, nurturing the faith and leadership of young parishioners. The unit organizes regular prayer meetings, social service activities, cultural programs, and sports events.

Open to all youth aged 14–35. Join us and grow in faith, friendship, and service.

  • ✦Monthly meetings after Sunday 9 AM Mass
  • ✦Annual camp and retreat
  • ✦Blood donation, charity drives
  • ✦Feast & Christmas celebrations

Catechism — Faith Formation

📖

Primary Classes

Grades 1–4 — Introduction to prayer, Bible stories, and the sacraments. Every Saturday 9:00–10:00 AM.

✝️

Junior Classes

Grades 5–7 — Deeper study of the Creed, Commandments, and First Communion preparation.

📿

Senior Classes

Grades 8–10 — Confirmation preparation, Church history, and moral theology fundamentals.

🎓

Higher Classes

Grades 11–12 — Advanced Scripture, ethics, and lay apostolate formation for young adults.

📅 Catechism Schedule

Every Saturday from 9:00 AM to 11:00 AM. Classes are held at the Parish Hall. Enrollment at the start of each academic year (June). Contact the catechism coordinator for details.

Pious Associations

Devout groups fostering prayer and devotion within the parish.

🌹

Legion of Mary

Devoted to the Blessed Virgin Mary, the Legion engages in prayer, visitation of the sick, and evangelization. Weekly meetings at the parish.

❤️‍🔥

Sacred Heart Sodality

Promotes devotion to the Sacred Heart of Jesus, First Friday Masses, and acts of reparation and love.

🕯️

St. Vincent de Paul Society

Serves the poor and marginalized through charity visits, food distribution, and financial assistance to needy families.

🎶

Parish Choir

Leads worship through sacred music at all Masses and special occasions. Open to all parishioners with a love for singing.

👴

Elders' Fellowship

Supports and cares for the elderly parishioners through monthly gatherings, home visits, and spiritual support.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Apostolate

Strengthens family bonds through retreats, counseling support, and shared faith-building activities.
