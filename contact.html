Frequently Asked

🚗

How to Reach Us

From Muvattupuzha town: Take the road towards Mekkadambu. The church is located at Mekkadambu P.O., approximately 8 km from town. Auto-rickshaws and buses available.

📋

Parish Registration

New families joining the parish must register at the parish office. Download the membership form from our Downloads page.

🕊️

Sacrament Enquiries

For Baptism, Marriage, First Communion, or other sacraments, visit the parish office during working hours or call in advance.

🆘

Emergency Services

For anointing of the sick or last rites in an emergency, contact the Vicar directly at any time. The phone number is available from the parish office.
